Important Information:

The conference proceedings will be submitted for inclusion in the IEEE Interactive Electronic Library (IEL) as part of IEEE Xplore. All authors who have been accepted must register for the conference and attend the conference to present their paper. Authors who do not attend the conference to present their papers, or arrange for a co-author or knowledgeable colleague to present their paper in the absence of the primary author, will not have their paper published in the IEEE IEL and IEEE Xplore, per IEEE policy. IEEE reserves the right to exclude a submission from distribution after the conference, including exclusion from IEEE Xplore, if the submission does not meet IEEE standards for scope and/or quality. Articles excluded from further distribution shall be archived by IEEE but shall not be indexed or appear on IEEE Xplore.

Cancellation Policy:

In principle, the early registration cannot be cancelled after the due date of early registration. The late registration cannot be cancelled after July 15. There is also a 20% fee charged for the cancellations. For non-author registrations, if the invitation letter has been sent upon the attendee's request, the NVMSA 2017 conference organizing committee reserves the right to reject the cancellation. The regulation is to prevent possible fraudulency for an invitation letter to enter Taiwan. Please note that due to financial handling procedure, all refund will be made after the conference.
